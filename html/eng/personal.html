Personal Projects

Sewing

Once I started my studies, I learnt about the concept of sewing and designing your own clothes. Ever since then I've had a great time learning to sew. Most of my knowledge is from the internet.
I sewed this pinafore dress following a YouTube tutorial by Annika Victoria.

During the first corona lockdown, I studied sewing patterns from the 1940s based on a book in my familie's posession. There are no sewing instructions for any of the patterns, so I had to try to find my own path to a wearable result.
Firstly I've made a mock up of all pieces out of thrifted bedsheets, so I could test the fit and the construction methods. I've tried some techniques for the first time like button plackets, collars and lining.
It is very interesting to see how things were cut differently in the 1940s compared to today. For example, the back panel of the skirt is longer, which ensures a leveled hem all around.

Art

Unlike sewing, I've been drawing for most of my life. My last art lesson might have been more than five years back, however I never stoped drawing. All drawings shown here are entirely traditional, which means they were made with pencil and paper.
I enjoy drawing and writing the most with hard pencils.

Some years back I attempted Inktober. The goal of Inktober is to draw something with ink every day according to a prompt.
This drawing is for one of the prompts. I put this drawing aside for a long time, because I wrinkled the paper during the sketching phase. Over the course of the following 6 months, I worked on it every now and then with some thin ink pens until I've reached this result.
I'm still very proud of this drawing, despite it being about two years old.

What I like the most about drawing is coloring. It's a very time consuming step and works best with daylight, which is way I don't do it that often.
It's always fascinating to see which vibrant colors can be achieved by layering different pencils.

Conclusion

Although I pratice my hobbies solely in my free time rather than on a professional level, I still enjoy doing them a lot.
As you can tell, I also enjoy creating and learning something new not only in my studies but also in my free time.
